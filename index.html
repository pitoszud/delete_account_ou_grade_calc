Instructions for deleting OU Grade Calculator account

Open OU Grade Calculator app, and open a drawer.
Next, go to Settings
In Settings, tap on "Delete Account".
You will see a prompt window, where you have to confirm the deletion of your account.

Please note - all modules you saved will be deleted permanently and your account will be removed.
Thank you for using the app.
